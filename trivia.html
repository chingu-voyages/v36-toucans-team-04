Fun Facts

Christopher Sholes designed the QWERTY keyboard we use today in 1872. This design has been used for 150 years. Ironically, this is not the most efficient keyboard layout.

Keyboard Design

QWERTY
Developed in 1936 by an American psychologist, the Dvorak layout was designed to minimize finger movements to cause less strain and fewer errors. Unfortunately, by this time, the QWERTY design has already been widespread.
The spacebar is the most commonly used key on your keyboard. 18% of all strokes on a keyboard is to use the spacebar key.

The most commonly used key

spacebar
In fact, when you hit spacebar, 600,000 people around the world did just the same at any moment.
The average wpm is around 41.4 words in one minute.

The average accuracy for a typist is around 92%, which is around 8 mistakes for every 100 words typed.
