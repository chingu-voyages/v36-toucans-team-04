Welcome to Falling Words

The game that increases your typing speed!!!

The goal

Type as many words as you can

Typing a word correctly gives you points

Misstyping a word penalizes you taking a live away

Typebox (typing correctly)

Type box shows the characters that you are typing. If the text inside type box is matching the prefix of a word then it has a green background. The word inside the game also gets highlighted.

Typebox (mistyped)

If you misstype one or more characters typebox show a red background behind those characters. The word inside the game is not highlighted unless you correct those mistyped characters.

In-game information

The game shows current game difficulty, your current score, typing speed and remaining lives

Bonus words

Blue words are bonus words. They are considerably faster than normal ones and if you type one of them correctly you get a live. You can ignore them as they don't penalize you when reaching the bottom of the screen.
